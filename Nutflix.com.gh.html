✦ Our Story

Rooted in Passion,
Ground with Purpose

NUTFLIX is an innovative e-business focused on producing and selling premium peanut butter made primarily from cashew nuts and groundnuts. The brand aims to redefine local peanut butter by introducing unique flavors and high-quality processing standards.

Concept

From a Family Recipe to a Beloved Brand

NUTFLIX specializes in locally produced, nutritious, and flavorful peanut butter. The product line includes: - Nutflix original (raw, natural peanut butter) - Chocolate Nut - Honey Nut - Spicy Nut (upcoming) The use of cashew nuts gives Nutflix a distinctive taste and premium positioning in the market The primary motivation behind Nutflix is to create a unique, healthy, and locally made product that stands out in a competitive market. It also aims to promote local agriculture, create employment, and provide consumers with nutritious alternatives.>

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every decision we make comes back to three things.

🌱

Radical Honesty

No hidden ingredients. No marketing spin. Our label says exactly what's inside — peanuts, sea salt, and nothing else. We'll never compromise on that.

🤝

Community First

We source 100% of our peanuts from smallholder farmers across northern Ghana, paying fair prices that go directly into local families and livelihoods.

🔬

Craft Over Speed

We slow-roast in small batches and stone-grind every jar by hand. It takes twice as long. It tastes three times as good. We're fine with that trade-off.

From Farm to Jar

Our Process

Four careful steps, zero shortcuts.

🌾

Sourcing

We hand-select premium Ghanaian peanuts at peak harvest from our trusted network of farmers.

🔥

Slow Roasting

Each batch is drum-roasted low and slow to unlock the deepest, most complex flavour possible.

⚙️

Grinding and Blending

We grind and blend different flaours that preserve the natural oils and give our paste its signature texture.

🫙

Packaging

Every jar is filled, sealed, and labelled by hand before being shipped directly to your door.
